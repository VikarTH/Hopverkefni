Overwatch was announced on November 7th, 2014. Released on May 23rd, 2016.

Development of Overwatch was started when the cancellation of the game Titan came to be. The Titan development team
got cut into a smaller group and began working on Overwatch. 

Developer: Blizzard Entertainment
Publisher: Blizzard Entertainment
Directors: Jeffrey Kaplan, Chris Metzen, Aaron Keller
Designers: Jeremy Craig, Michael Elliott, Scott Mercer
Programmers: Mike Elliott, John LeFleur
Artists: William Petras, Arnold Tsang
Composer: Derek Duke, Neal Acree
